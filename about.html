About

My name is Peter Chang, and I am a Graphic Designer, specializing in futuristic vector art. I’m a big fan of cyberpunk art, music, and video games. I am also a huge dog lover with two dogs of my own. My goal is to implement my inspirations artistically, from my perspective, and my understanding of science and math. I am attracted to many different forms of art and you will often find me photographing artistic pieces of interest to learn from and keep my art fresh.

I obtained my Graphic Design B.A. at CSU East Bay in Hayward, CA, and will be attending the Interaction Design Master’s program in the fall. I have experience working with the entire Adobe Suite, specializing in Adobe Illustrator and Indesign. I use a lot of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for coding websites. Currently a Personal Assistant and Graphic Designer to Fiona Ma, California State Treasurer, and I also work as a Student Assistant in the CSUEB Astronomy Lab. With these skills I will create various inventions and manufacture a tangible cyberspace for the entire world to experience.
